About Me

I am Shi Wang, Associate Professor from Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China. He is mainly engaged in the fields of knowledge graph, cognitive computing and natural language processing. Specifically, he focused on integrateing symbolic knowledge and deep learning to realize dual process cognitive computing. His paper was published in AAAI, WWW, EMNLP, ACL and other international top conferences, and has been applied in many ways.

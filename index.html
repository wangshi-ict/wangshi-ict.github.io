About Me

I am Shi Wang, Associate Professor from Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China. I am mainly engaged in the fields of knowledge graph, cognitive computing and natural language processing. Specifically, I focuse on integrateing symbolic knowledge and deep learning to realize dual process cognitive computing. My papers were published in AAAI, WWW, EMNLP, ACL and other international top conferences.
